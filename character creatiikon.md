Building a character in the B.R.U.T.A.L. Engine is designed to be a front-loaded architectural process. As a systems designer, I love this approach: you force the players to do all the heavy mathematical lifting during Session Zero. Once they construct their "Biological Chassis," the math is permanently baked into their physical dashboards, ensuring zero arithmetic slows down the actual 3-Beat Pulse of combat. 

Here is the complete, sequential data pipeline you need to guide a player through building their character. 

### Step 1: The Biological Chassis (Base Stats)
Players do not roll for stats; they select a predetermined matrix. They must choose one of the six biological Kingdoms: **Mammals, Reptiles/Amphibians, Avians, Aquatics, Insects/Arthropods, or Plants/Myconids**. 

Within that Kingdom, they select a Mechanical Sub-Type to ensure niche protection:
*   **T1 (The Balancer):** Stable, rounded arrays.
*   **T2 (The Heavy):** Dense entities focused on Might and physical durability.
*   **T3 (The Predator):** High-speed organisms optimized for reflexes.
*   **T4 (The Specialist):** Twitchy entities with superior technical or mental processing.
*(Crucial Note: T0 Origin stats are strictly for the Game Master to build mundane beasts and NPCs. Players must select T1-T4).*

### Step 2: Biological Origin (Fictional Positioning)
Players select one of the 24 specific animal/plant origins within their chosen Kingdom (e.g., an Avian *Owl* or an Aquatic *Mantis Shrimp*). Each origin provides a single, highly specific passive mechanical ability that interacts directly with the master tag system, such as a Cockroach's total immunity to radiation, or an Eagle's ability to instantly detect a mundane lie. 

### Step 3: Genetic Variation & Life Experience
Players modify their base stats to reflect their specific history:
*   **Size Shift:** They may shift their Base Size one step. Shifting *down* grants +1 Finesse or +1 Reflexes; shifting *up* grants +1 Might or +1 Endurance.
*   **Life Experience:** They distribute exactly **3 Body points** and **3 Mind points** based on two narrative prompts: their Childhood/Environment and their Early Education/Trade. 

### Step 4: Professional Training (The 6 Skill Tracks)
Players lock in their combat optimization by selecting exactly 6 Skill Tracks (represented by oversized Tarot cards). This grants them a permanent **+2** to the governing attribute of each selected track. The selection must follow this strict architecture:
*   1 Body Assault Track & 1 Mind Assault Track.
*   1 Body Aegis Track & 1 Mind Aegis Track.
*   2 Active Arts (Martial Tactics or Anomalies).

**The Design Complication & Rule Enforcement:** 
*The Biological Ceiling.* By stacking Size Shifts, Life Experience, and six +2 track bonuses, players will inevitably try to min-max a stat to godhood. You must strictly enforce the Hard Cap: **No stat may ever exceed an 8 during character creation**. If a stat hits 9 or higher, the overflow must be bled into another stat of the same biological type (Body to Body, Mind to Mind). This mathematically guarantees well-rounded chassis and prevents the game engine from breaking.

### Step 5: The Resource Pools & Derived Stats
With the 12 Core Stats permanently locked, players calculate their physical and mental capacities:
*   **HP (Physical Health):** Endurance + Fortitude + Vitality.
*   **Composure (Mental Health):** Willpower + Logic + Charm.
*   **Stamina Capacity:** Might + Reflexes + Finesse.
*   **Focus Capacity:** Knowledge + Awareness + Intuition.

Next, they calculate the four Derived Tactical Sub-Stats that govern combat positioning and the Information Advantage (using the 2:1 ratio):
*   **Perception:** Awareness + Logic + Vitality.
*   **Stealth:** Knowledge + Charm + Finesse.
*   **Movement:** Reflexes + Might + Intuition.
*   **Balance:** Endurance + Fortitude + Willpower.

### Step 6: The 6-Piece Static Loadout & Gear Tax
Players select exactly 3 Physical items (Armor, Melee, Ranged) and exactly 3 Mental items (Headgear, Close Jewelry, Ranged Projector). Every piece of gear carries a Tax that subtracts from their Maximum Capacity: Light (-1), Medium (-2), or Heavy (-3). Light surgical weapons cost 0 Tax.

Subtracting the total Gear Tax from their Maximum Capacity yields their **Reserve Pool**.

5. Tactical Sub-Stats and Health Formula Appendix

Derived statistics use a 2:1 ratio to ensure narrative and mechanical parity.

Calculations

* HP (Physical Health Capacity): Endurance + Fortitude + Vitality.
* Composure (Mental Health Capacity): Willpower + Logic + Charm.
* Stamina Capacity: Might + Reflexes + Finesse.
* Focus Capacity: Knowledge + Awareness + Intuition.
* Perception: (2 Mind : 1 Body) — Awareness + Logic + Vitality.
* Stealth & Camo: (2 Mind : 1 Body) — Knowledge + Charm + Finesse.
* Movement & Speed: (2 Body : 1 Mind) — Reflexes + Might + Intuition.
* Balance & Sure-Footedness: (2 Body : 1 Mind) — Endurance + Fortitude + Willpower.

Action Battery

All combatants begin every encounter with a full 10/10 Stamina and 10/10 Focus Active Battery.

6. Professional Training & The Biological Ceiling

Selection Structure

Every character must select exactly 6 tracks:

1. 1 Body Assault + 1 Mind Assault.
2. 1 Body Aegis + 1 Mind Aegis.
3. 2 Active Arts (Martial Tactics or Anomalies).

The Bonus & Hard Cap

* Each selected track permanently adds +2 to its governing attribute.
* Hard Cap: No attribute may exceed 8 during character creation.
* Overflow Rule: Points exceeding 8 must be reallocated into a stat of the same type:
  * Body Stats: Might, Endurance, Finesse, Reflex, Vitality, Fortitude.
  * Mind Stats: Knowledge, Logic, Awareness, Intuition, Charm, Willpower.

7. Static Loadout & Gear Tax Reference

Equipping gear taxes the biological chassis. This tax is subtracted from the character's Maximum Capacity to determine the Reserve Pool.

Tier	Item Type	Resource Tax (S-Die/F-Die)	Armor Mod (Damage Reduction)
Light	Silks / Daggers*	-1 Tax	1 Armor Mod
Medium	Chain / Hammers	-2 Tax	2 Armor Mod
Heavy	Plate / Greatswords	-3 Tax	3 Armor Mod

*Note: Light Weapons cost exactly -0 Tax to equip.

Tactical Loadout Rules

* 50% Threshold Rule: If the total Gear Tax exceeds 50% of Maximum Biological Capacity, Action Die regeneration drops from 2 tokens per turn to 1.
* Shock of Loss: If armor/wards are stripped mid-combat, the Tax remains. Adrenaline and physiological shock prevent pool recalculation until a Full Rest is completed.
