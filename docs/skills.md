THE B.R.U.T.A.L. ENGINE: COMPLETE SKILL TRACK COMPENDIUM
This document contains the finalized, fully updated mechanics for all 48 Skill Tracks, stripped of outdated drafts. It is divided into three sections: The Passive Hardware (Assault & Aegis), The Active Martial Tactics (Subconscious Channeling), and The Anomalies (Celestial Clockwork Magic).
PART I: THE PASSIVE HARDWARE (ASSAULT & AEGIS)
These tracks use Base Action Economy. They contain no active skills to trigger; they permanently upgrade standard "Move," "Strike," and "Defend" actions to eliminate cognitive load.
The Universal Passive Template
T1 (The Stance): Minor positioning/tactical perk added to standard actions.
T2 (Loadout Efficiency): Permanent -1 to Max Stamina/Focus gear cost.
T3 (Action Multiplier): Action economy bump (e.g., free secondary target/movement).
T4 (Penetration/Synergy): Basic actions ignore specific defenses or penalties.
T5 (The Reaction): Upgrades passive out-of-turn capabilities.
T6 (Master Loadout): Additional -1 Max Stamina/Focus gear cost (Total -2).
T7 (Status Application): Standard actions automatically apply a Status Tag.
T8 (Flow State): A triggered reset granting a free Action/Move Beat.
T9 (Grandmaster): Permanent state that breaks a fundamental combat rule.
